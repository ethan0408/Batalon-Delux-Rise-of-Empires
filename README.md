# ⚔ Rise of Empires — *Batalon Delux*

A 1700s real-time grand-strategy game set on the fictional continent of **Aurca**. Lead one of seven powers — Carak, Ostana, Illyria, Rigan, Aveon, Javein, or the corsair isle of Pirati — and conquer the continent through war, diplomacy, intrigue, and statecraft. Built as a single, self-contained HTML file — no installs, no dependencies.


## Features
- **Isometric 3D map** with rotation, named provinces & cities, terrain (mountains, hills, forest, marsh, rivers), seasons, and winter attrition.
- **Seven unique nations** with their own ideologies, leaders, lore, and rivalries — plus breakaway states (Cruv, Mangobi).
- **Huge national focus trees** (38–43 nodes each) with secret paths and easter eggs.
- **Deep economy** — gold, knowledge, recruits, political power; manufactories, arsenals, strategic resources (saltpetre, iron, horses, timber), trade routes, and province development.
- **Cabinet & laws** — appoint ministers and pass economy/conscription laws.
- **Real combat** — organization vs strength, combined arms, terrain combat-width, supply & encirclement, generals with portraits & histories.
- **Land, sea & air** — line infantry, grenadiers, artillery, cavalry, frigates & ships-of-the-line, and aeronaut corps; naval blockades, escorts & interception, and air superiority.
- **Living world** — casus belli & justified wars, alliances called to arms, vassals & tribute, gradual covert uprisings, stability & rebellions, interactive event decisions, and a full war **chronicle**.
- **Quality of life** — title screen, tutorial, battle reports, a live dispatches feed, save/load, and a domination victory.

## How to play
Pick a nation, grow your economy and army, advance a national focus, earn a *casus belli*, then conquer. **Hold 70% of the map** (or be the last power standing) to win. Full instructions are in the in-game **❓ How to Play** screen and tutorial.

**Controls:** click an army → click a province to march · **Shift-click** = send all armies · **A** = cycle armies · hover a province for details · **Q/E** or arrow keys rotate · Space pauses.

## Hosting it as a live link (GitHub Pages)
1. Push these files to a GitHub repo (instructions below).
2. In the repo: **Settings → Pages → Build and deployment → Source: Deploy from a branch → `main` / root → Save.**
3. After a minute your game is live at `https://<your-username>.github.io/<repo-name>/`.
